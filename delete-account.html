Delete your account and data

Developer: FitChecker · App: FitChecker

You can permanently delete your FitChecker account and everything stored with it, at any time. There are two ways to do it.

Delete it yourself, inside the app

  1. Open FitChecker and make sure you are logged in.
  2. Go to More → Settings.
  3. Tap “Delete my account & data.”
  4. Confirm. Your account and all associated data are erased immediately.

Or ask us to delete it for you

If you can’t access the app, email us from the address on your account and we’ll delete everything for you:

Account deletion requests: andypang122@gmail.com
Please send from the email address tied to your account. We complete deletion requests within 30 days, and usually within a few days.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes, from both your device and our servers:

  • Your login (email address and password hash)
  • Your saved body profiles and measurements
  • Your wardrobe items and any photos you added
  • Your favourited products
  • Your saved outfits

This action cannot be undone. Community posts you chose to publish are removed on request — use the report tool in the app or email us at the address above.

For more on how we handle data, see our Privacy Policy.
