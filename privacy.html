Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

FitChecker helps you understand how a garment will fit before you buy it. We built it to keep your personal information on your own device. This policy explains exactly what stays local, what is sent over the internet, and why.

What stays on your device

The following is stored only in your browser / app on this device and is never uploaded to us:

  • Your account details (name, email address, and a scrambled hash of your password — never the password itself)
  • Body measurements and any profiles you create (for family members, etc.)
  • Photos of garments you analyze
  • Your size history and app settings (units, language)

Clearing your browser data, or using “Delete my account & data” in Settings, permanently erases all of it.

What is sent over the internet — and only when you use that feature

  • Interface translation. When you choose a language, the on-screen text is sent to a translation service to be translated. Your name, email, and measurements are never included.
  • Size-guide reader. If you paste a brand’s size-guide link, that web address is fetched and read so we can pull out the measurement chart.
  • Community outfits. If you choose to post an outfit, the photo, caption, and display name you enter are uploaded for review. Once a moderator approves it, it is shown publicly in the app. A style score is calculated on your device, not by us.

Community content, moderation & retention

Every outfit post is reviewed by a person before it appears. Anyone can report a post; reported posts are hidden immediately and re-reviewed. We remove content that breaks our Community Guidelines and may block users who violate them. Approved posts remain visible until you or a moderator removes them. To have a post you made taken down, use the report tool or contact us at the address below.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not run third-party advertising or behavioural tracking inside the app.
  • “You’d like this” may contain affiliate links to retailers; if you buy through one we may earn a commission, but we do not track your activity on those sites.

Children

FitChecker is not directed at children. You must be at least 13 years old (or the minimum age required in your country) to create an account. Measurement profiles for children are entered and managed by the responsible adult account holder.

Your choices

  • Delete everything on your device at any time via Settings → “Delete my account & data”.
  • Request removal of any community post you made by contacting us.
  • Stop using internet features (translation, size-guide reader, community) and the app still works for fit checks.

Contact

Questions or removal requests: [andypang122@gmail.com]. We aim to respond within a few days, and to act on reported objectionable content within 24 hours.
